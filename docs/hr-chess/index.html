High Roller Chess Rules

This game follows most of the same rules of chess. However, when you want to capture a piece, you must specify which piece you are moving, then roll the die.

The number shown on the die is the maximum number of times that piece can move.

You may then move that piece, and your turn ends once you make a move that does not capture an opponent’s piece, or when you reach the maximum number of moves for that turn.

About the Embedded Chess Board

This is a freeplay chess board with multiplayer functionality; you move pieces without the typical chess board restrictions and connect with others by creating and joining rooms.
